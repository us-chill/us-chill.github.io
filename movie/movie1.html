Action Heroes (2024)

Genre: Action, Adventure — Year: 2024

Synopsis

This high-octane blockbuster follows a team of unlikely heroes as they race to stop a global threat. Packed with explosive stunts, thrilling chases, and surprising heart, Action Heroes delivers non-stop entertainment.

Review

USChill's review: The film leans heavily on spectacle but delivers charismatic performances and memorable set-pieces. Fans of classic action will find it satisfying.
